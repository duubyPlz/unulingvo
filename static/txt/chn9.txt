== Greetings Three ==
    O: 你們都很忙。
    E: You are all busy.
    F: You _are all busy_.

    O: 早上好！
    E: Good morning (mainland chinese)!

    O: 我早上很忙。
    E: I am busy in the morning.
    F: _I_ am busy in the morning.

    O: 你忙嗎？
    E: Are you busy?

    O: 他早上不喝茶。
    E: He does not drink tea in the morning.

    O: 我不忙。
    E: I am not busy.

> Two
    O: 你今天怎麼樣？
    E: How are you today?

    O: 你們今天怎麼樣？
    E: How are you all today?

    O: 她們怎麼樣？
    E: How are they[f]?

    O: 我今天很好。
    E: I am good today.

    O: 李老師今天不高興。
    E: Teacher Li is not happy today.

    O: 他怎麼樣？
    E: How is he?

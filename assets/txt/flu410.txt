== TTMIK 4.10 - Among, Between / 사이에, 사이에서, 중에, 중에서 ==
* w -> with
* s -> short
* l -> long

    # Meanings:
    # * (중):
        * Adjective for sino nouns
        * In progress
        * Short for 중의 or 중에
    # * 중에서: Among countable options (not location)
    # * 종에: 
        * Short for 중에서
        * 종에[at] + verb
    # * (종의): 종의['s] + noun
    # * 사이에서: Among uncountable/abstract noun (e.g. popular among...)
    # * 사이에: Physical space between two things.

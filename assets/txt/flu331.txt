== TTMIK 3.31 - [Review] Level 3 ==
* s -> short

    O: 요즘 날씨가 너무 좋네요.
    E: (Wow) The weather is so (lit. too) good these days.
    F: _(Wow)_ The weather _is so (lit. too) good_ these days.

    O: 네, 날씨가 정말 좋은 것 같아요.
    E: Yes, I think the weather is really good.

    O: 저 내일 지민 씨하고 배드민턴 치는데, 같이 해요.
    E: (lit. But) I’m[s] playing badminton with Jimin tomorrow. Join us (lit. do it together).

    O: 내일요? 어디에서요?
    E: Tomorrow? Where?

    O: 제 사무실 앞에 공원이 있어요.
    E: There is a park in front of my[s] office.
    F: _There is_ a park _in front of my[s] office_.

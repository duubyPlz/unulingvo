	E: Hello.

> Two
    O: 네.
    E: Yes.

    O: 아니요.
    E: No.

	O: 커피 좋아해요?
	E: Do you like coffee?

	O: 네. 좋아해요.
	E: Yes, I like it.

	O: 커피 안 좋아해요?
	E: You don’t like coffee?

	O: 아니요. 좋아해요.
	E: No, I like it.

	O: 네. 안 좋아해요.
	E: Yes, I don't like it.

	O: 네, 맞아요.
	E: Yes, that is right.

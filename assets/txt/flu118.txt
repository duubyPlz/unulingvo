== TTMIK 1.18 - Location Particles / 에, 에서 ==
    # Whereas it is POSSIBLE to make sentences without location marking particles, by using the right location marking particles, you can make your message very clear. 

    O: 어디 가고 싶어요?
    E: Where do you want to go? (No location particle)

    O: 학교에 갔어요.
    E: I went to school.

    O: 한국에 왔어요.
    E: I came to Korea.

    O: 어디에 가고 싶어요?
    E: Where do you want to go?

    O: 지금 어디에 있어요?
    E: Where are you now? 

    O: 사무실에
    E: to the office, to an office, to my office

    O: 사무실에서
    E: from the office, from an office, from my office

    O: 집에 있어요.
    E: I am at home.

    O: 집에 가요.
    E: I am going home.

    O: 사무실에 있어요.
    E: I am at the office.

    O: 집에서 일해요.
    E: I work at home.

    O: 집에서 뭐 해요? 
    E: What are you doing at home?

    O: 사무실에서 일해요.
    E: I work at the office.

== TTMIK 4.8 - May I? / May I…, Don't have to / It is okay to…, You don’t have to… / -아/어/여도 돼요, 해도 돼요 ==
* s -> short
* l -> long

    # {#RELATED Level 2 Lesson 20}: -아/어/여야 되다/하다

    # * Meaning:
    #   * It's okay to...
    #   * You don't need to...

    # Variations:
    #   * -도 되다: -may
    #   * -안 + verb + -도 되다: -don't have to
    #       > Note: Using -안 is not "you may not". 
    # {#RELATED Level 4 Lesson 9}: -면 안 돼요

    # -도 Variations:
    #   * Noun + -도: -too
    #   * Verb + -도: Even if-

    # 되다: { to function/work, to be possible }

    O: 지금 인터넷 돼요?
    E: Does the Internet work now?

    O: 지금 배달 돼요?
    E: Can you deliver (the food) now? / Is food delivery available now?

    O: 배달 돼요?
    E: Do you deliver (food)? / Can food delivery be done?

    # Noun + -도
    O: 저도요.
    E: Me, too.

    O: 이것도 주세요.
    E: Give me this[l] one, too.

    # Verb + -도
    O: 먹어도
    E: even if you eat

    O: 몰라도
    E: even if you do not know

    O: 자도
    E: even if you sleep

    O: 일어나도
    E: even when you wake up

    # 켜다 - to turn/switch on
    O: 켜도 되다
    E: to be okay to turn … on

    O: 켜도 돼요.
    E: It is okay to turn on. / It is okay. You can turn it on.

    O: 불 켜도 돼요.
    E: You can turn the light on.

    O: 컴퓨터 켜도 돼요. 
    E: You can turn the computer on.

    O: 해도 되다
    E: to be okay to do …

    O: 내일 해도 돼요.
    E: It is okay if you do it tomorrow. / You can just do it tomorrow.

    O: 내일 해도 돼요?
    E: Is it okay if I do it tomorrow?

    O: 시작해도 돼요.
    E: You can start.

    O: 시작해도 돼요? 
    E: Can I start? / Can we start?

    O: 이거 나중에 해도 돼요.
    E: You can do this[s] later.

    O: 이거 나중에 해도 돼요?
    E: Can I do this[s] later?

    O: 컴퓨터 써도 돼요?
    E: May I use the computer?

    O: 오늘 쉬어도 돼요.
    E: You can take a day off today.

    # Don't have to
    O: 안 해도 돼요.
    E: You do not have to do it. / It is okay if you do not do it.

    O: 청소 안 해도 돼요.
    E: You do not have to clean up. / It is okay if you do not clean up.

    O: 오늘 일 안 해도 돼요.
    E: You do not have to work today. / It is okay if you do not work today.

    O: 안 먹어도 돼요. 
    E: You do not have to eat it. / It is okay if you do not eat it.

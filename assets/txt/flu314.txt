== TTMIK 3.14 - Adjectives Action / Making Adjectives Action Verbs + -는/(으)ㄴ/(으)ㄹ + 명사 ==
* s -> short

    # Adjective clause -> modifies noun.

    O: 노래하는 사람
    E: (the/a) person who sings

    O: 좋아하는 책 
    E: (the/a) book that I like

    O: 가다. 가는.
    E: To go. That's going (adjective)

    O: 자다. 자는.
    E: To sleep. That is sleeping (adjective).

    O: 열다. 여는.
    E: To open. That is opening (adjective).

    O: 불다. 부는.
    E: To blow. That is blown (adjective).

    O: 민지가 좋아하는 사람
    E: someone who Minji likes

    O: 민지를 좋아하는 사람
    E: someone who likes Minji

    O: 이 노래는 제가 좋아하는 노래예요.
    E: This song is a song that I like.
    F: _This song_ is a song that I like.

    O: 자주 먹는 한국 음식 있어요?
    E: Is there a Korean food that you eat often?

    O: 자주 가는 카페 있어요?
    E: Is there a cafe that you go to often?

    O: 요즘 좋아하는 가수는 누구예요?
    E: Which singer do you like these days?
    F: Which _singer_ do you _like these days_?

    O: 요즘 공부하고 있는 외국어는 일본어예요.
    E: The foreign language I am studyING these days is Japanese.
    F: _The foreign language I am studyING these days_ is Japanese.

    O: 눈이 오는 날에는 영화 보고 싶어요.
    E: On a day when it snows, I want to see a movie[s].
    F: _On a day when it snows_, I want to see a movie[s].

    O: 저기 있는 사람, 아는 사람이에요?
    E: That person who is over there, is it someone that you know?

    # Anybody (who is) hungry?
    O: 배고픈 사람 (있어요)?
    E: (Is there) anyone that's hungry?

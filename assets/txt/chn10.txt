== Location One ==
> One
    O: 北京在中國。
    E: Beijing is in China.

    O: 我的家在北京。
    E: My home is in Beijing.

    O: 老師不在家。
    E: The teacher is not home.

    O: 醫生是北京人。
    E: The doctor is from Beijing.

    O: 你的家在美國嗎？
    E: Is your home in the US?

    O: 她們在英國嗎？
    E: Are they in the UK?

    O: 他的家在中國。
    E: His home is in China.

> Two
    O: 紐約在美國。
    E: New York is in the US.

    O: 他的家在香港那裏？
    E: Where in Hong Kong is his home?

    O: 你的香港電話號碼是多少？
    E: What's your Hong Kong phone number?

    O: 你們在那裏？
    E: Where are you all?

    O: 你的家在那裏？
    E: Where is your home?

    O: 我們的家在紐約。
    E: Our home is in New York.

    O: 我的醫生是紐約人。
    E: My doctor is from New York.

> Three
    O: 你的醫生住在倫敦嗎？
    E: Does your doctor live in London?

    O: 我們的家在台灣。
    E: Our home is in Taiwan.

    O: 她住在台灣。
    E: She lives in Taiwan.

    O: 你的老師住在台灣嗎？
    E: Does your teacher live in Taiwan?

    O: 倫敦在英國。
    E: London is in the UK.

    O: 我的學生住在倫敦。
    E: My student lives in London.

    O: 王老師住在倫敦那裏？
    E: Where in London does Teacher Wang live?

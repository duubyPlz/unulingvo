== TTMIK 3.29 - ㅅ irregular / ㅅ 불규칙 ==
    # Irregular: ㅅ 받침 + vowel ➔ drop ㅅ (for 아|어)
    # Regular: Leave the ㅅ (for 아|어)

    O: 낫다. 나아요.
    E: To heal / to recover / to be better. It is better. / Please feel better.

    O: 젓다. 저어요
    E: To stir (liquid). I stir. / Please stir it.

    O: 잇다. 이었어요.
    E: To connect / to link. I connected it. / I linked it.

    O: 짓다. 지었어요.
    E: To build / to compose. I built it. / I composed it.

    O: 잘 저으세요.
    E: Stir it well.

    O: 두 개를 이었어요.
    E: I connected the two (objects).

    O: 이 집을 누가 지었어요?
    E: Who built this house?
    F: Who _built this house_?

    O: 좋은 이름을 지을 거예요.
    E: I will create a good name.

    O: 감기 다 나았어요?
    E: Did you recover completely from the cold?

    O: 웃다. 웃어요.
    E: To smile / to laugh. Smile. / I smile. / He laughs. / They laugh.

    O: 씻다. 씻을 거예요. 
    E: To wash. I am going to wash up. / I am going to wash it.

    O: 벗다. 신발을 벗어 주세요.
    E: To take (clothes) off. Please take your shoes off.

== TTMIK 3.29 - ㅅ irregular / ㅅ 불규칙 ==
* s -> short

    # Irregular: ㅅ 받침 + vowel ➔ drop ㅅ (for 아|어)
    # Regular: Leave the ㅅ (for 아|어)

    # Please feel better.
    O: 낫다. 나아요.
    E: To heal / to recover / to be better. It is better.

    # Please stir it.
    O: 젓다. 저어요
    E: To stir (liquid). I stir.

    O: 잇다. 이었어요.
    E: To connect / to link. I connected it. / I linked it.

    O: 짓다. 지었어요.
    E: To build / to compose. I built it. / I composed it.

    O: 젓다. 잘 저으세요.
    E: To stir (liquid). Stir it well.

    O: 잇다. 두 개를 이었어요.
    E: To connect. I connected the two (objects).

    O: 짓다. 이 집을 누가 지었어요?
    E: To build / to compose. Who built this house?
    F: To build / to compose. Who _built_ this house?

    # I will create a good name.
    O: 짓다. 좋은 이름을 지을 거예요.
    E: To compose. I will compose a good name.

    O: 감기 다 나았어요?
    E: Did you recover completely from the cold[s]?

    O: 웃다. 웃어요.
    E: To smile / to laugh. Smile. / I smile. / He laughs. / They laugh.

    O: 씻다. 씻을 거예요. 
    E: To wash. I am going to wash up. / I am going to wash it.

    O: 벗다. 신발을 벗어 주세요.
    E: To take (clothes) off. Please take your shoes off.

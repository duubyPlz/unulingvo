== Introduction to Particles ==
    O: 学校はどこ？
    E: Where is school?

    O: それはどうして？
    E: Why is that?

    O: ミーティングはいつ？
    E: When is meeting?

    O: これは何？
    E: What is this?

    O: 映画はどう？
    E: How is movie?

    O: 彼は中学生だ。
    E: He is middle school student.

    O: 彼女は先生だ。
    E: She is teacher.

    O: 今日は雨。
    E: Today is rain.

    O: ボブは友達。
    E: Bob is friend.

    O: 彼は知り合い？
    E: Is he an acquaintance?

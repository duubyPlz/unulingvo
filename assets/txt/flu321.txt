== TTMIK 3.21 - Linking Verbs with -는데 / noun + -인데, adjective + -ㄴ데 ==
* s -> short

    # * -는데: action verbs
    # * -은/ㄴ데 descriptive verb
    #   * -ㄴ: vowel, ㄹ (ㄹ gets dropped), 이다 & 아니다
    # * -인데: noun (noun + 있다 + -ㄴ데 ?)

    # Usages (용법):
    # 1. Context -> [question|occurred]
    # 2. Juxtaposition
    # 3. Exclamation
    # 4. -는데?$ Question waiting for their explanation

    O: 하는데
    E: 하다

    O: 있는데
    E: 있다

    O: 먹는데
    E: 먹다

    O: 작은데
    E: 작다

    O: 좁은데
    E: 좁다

    O: 예쁜데
    E: 예쁘다

    O: 먼데
    E: 멀다

    # -그레서- but that sounds a bit unnatural
    O: 내일 일요일인데, 뭐 할 거예요?
    E: It is Sunday tomorrow + what are you going to do?

    O: 어제 자고 있었는데, 한국에서 전화가 왔어요. 
    E: I was sleeping (continuous) yesterday + I got a phone call from Korea (lit: a call from Korea came).

    O: 아직 9시인데 벌써 졸려요.
    E: It is still 9 o’clock but I am already sleepy.

    O: 준비 많이 했는데요...
    E: I prepared{s} a lot, but. (sentence ends)

    O: 멋있는데요!
    E: Oh, (that) is cool!

    O: 지금 어디에 있는데요?
    E: So where are you now? (Explain yourself)

    O: 지금 어디에 있는데 늦어요?
    E: So where are you now, that you're late? (Explain yourself)

    O: 지금요? 지금 바쁜데요.
    E: Now? I am busy now, so...

    O: 지금? 지금 바쁜데.
    E: Now? I am busy now, so... @impolite

    O: 지금 어디에 있는데?
    E: So where are you now? (Explain yourself) @impolite

    O: 멋있는데!
    E: Oh, (that) is cool! @impolite

    O: 준비 많이 했는데...
    E: I prepared{s} a lot, but. (sentence ends) @impolite

    O: 내일 친구 생일인데, 선물을 아직 못 샀어요.
    E: It is (my) friend’s{s} birthday tomorrow, but I still haven't bought a present.

    O: 이거 일본에서 샀는데, 선물이에요.
    E: I bought this in Japan, and it is a present for you.

    O: 오늘 뉴스에서 봤는데, 그거 진짜예요?
    E: I saw it in the news today. Is that for real?

    O: 이거 좋은데요!
    E: This is good! / I like this!

    O: 어? 여기 있었는데.
    E: Huh? It was here{s}... @impolite

    O: 영화 재미있었는데, 무서웠어요.
    E: The movie{s} was interesting, but it was scary.

    O: 영화 봤는데, 무서웠어요.
    E: I saw a movie{s}, and it was scary.

    O: 저 지금 학생인데, 일도 하고 있어요.
    E: I{s} am a student now, but I am also working (continuous).

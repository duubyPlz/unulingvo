== TTMIK 3.5 - Approximately, About / 쯤, 약, 정도 ==
    # -쯤, 정도 - after nouns
    # 약 - BEFORE nouns
    # 정도 - has space before it, 쯤 does not

    O: 한 시쯤
    E: Around one o’clock

    O: 천 원쯤
    E: About one thousand won

    O: 한 달쯤
    E: About one month

    O: 4킬로미터쯤 
    E: About 4 kilometers

    O: 한국에 한 달쯤 있을 거예요.
    E: I will be in Korea for about one month.

    # A bit more formal
    # Sometimes people also use 약 and 쯤 together or 약 and 정도 together.

    O: 약 한 달
    E: Approximately a month

    O: 한 달 정도
    E: About a month (extent)

    O: 약 한 달 정도
    E: Approximately a month (extent)

    O: 약 한 달쯤
    E: Approximately about a month

    # 백명쯤 왔어요.
    O: 백 명쯤 왔어요.
    E: About one hundred people came.

    O: 독일에서 이년쯤 살았어요.
    E: I lived in Germany for about two years.

    O: 한국에서 삼십 년쯤 살았어요.
    E: I lived in Korea for about thirty years.

    O: 언제쯤 갈 거예요?
    E: About when are you going to go?

    O: 내일 몇 시쯤 만날까요?
    E: (Shall:) Around what time shall we meet tomorrow?

    O: 다섯 시쯤 어때요?
    E: How about around five o’clock?

    O: 하루에 한국어 몇 시간쯤 공부해요?
    E: In a day, around how many hours do you study Korean?

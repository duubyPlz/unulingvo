== TTMIK 3.3 -  In front of, Behind, On top of, Under, Next to / 앞에, 옆에, 위에, 밑에, 뒤에 ==
* l -> long

    # ~에: more accurate, location-marking particle
    # 어디에 있어요? 
    O: 어디 있어요? 
    E: Where is it? / Where are you? / Where are they?

    O: 현우 씨, 어디에 있어요? 
    E: Hyunwoo, where[l] are you?

    O: 어디 있었어요?
    E: Where were you? / Where have you been?

    O: 어디 있을 거예요?
    E: Where will you be? / Where are you going to be?

    O: 친구를 어디에서 만날 거예요?
    E: Where are you going to meet (your) friends?

    O: 은행 앞에서 만날 거예요.
    E: I’m going to meet (them) in front of the bank.

    O: 은행 뒤에서 만날 거예요.
    E: I’m going to meet (them) behind the bank.

    O: 은행 옆에서 만날 거예요.
    E: I’m going to meet (them) beside the bank.

    O: 소파 위에서 자고 있어요.
    E: I’m sleeping on the sofa.

    O: 나무 밑에서 책을 읽고 있어요.
    E: I’m reading a book under the tree.

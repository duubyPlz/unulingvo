    # 今方 - Just a minute ago
    # * 조금 후에, 곧
    # * 조금 전에, 막
    # <https://hinative.com/questions/13721463>
    #
    <https://twitter.com/ttmik/status/1071612965994950656>
    # > 금방 is more colloquial and has a more “immediate” feel
    O: 금방 오실 거죠?
    E: You will be coming soon, right?

    O: 금방 사무실에서 나갔어요.
    E: (He's) just stepped out of the office. 

    O: 금방 돌아올게요
    E: I'll be right back.

    O: 방금 사당역을 지나쳤어요. 
    E: You just missed Sadang Station. 

    O: 그가 방금 내 차를 몰고 가 버렸어! 
    E: He just waltzed off with my car!

    O: 방금 보셨나요?
    E: Did you just see it? (Gently asking)

    O: 그들은 차를 몰고 왔다
    E: They came by car.

    O: 그는 차를 몰아 그녀의 집으로 갔다
    E: He drove to her house.

== 3. 얻다 to gain ==
* l -> long
* s -> short

    O: 용기를 얻다 
    E: to take courage

    O: 인기를 얻다
    E: to win popularity
 
    O: 명성을 얻다
    E: to win fame (lit: sound of name)

    O: 세력을 얻다
    E: to gain influence

    O: 얻다 놔뒀어?
    E: Did you leave it? @impolite

    O: 이거는 얻다 둬?
    E: Should we leave these? (lit. Is this to be left here?) @impolite
    F: Should we leave _these_? (lit. Is this to be left here?) @impolite

    O: 물을 얻어 마셔요.
    E: I got water{l} to drink.

    O: 오늘 많은 걸 얻어갔어요. 
    E: I learnt a lot today{s}. (Lit. Today I gained a lot of stuff.)

    # 몹시 terribly
    O: 새 차를 몹시 갖고 싶어.
    E: I badly want a new car. @impolite

    O: 동메달 획득했어요!
    E: I obtained a bronze medal!

    O: 큰 교혼을 하나 얻었습니다
    E: (We) learnt one big lesson. @formal

== TTMIK 2.15 - Only / -만 ==
* l -> long

    O: 이것만 살 거예요.
    E: I will only buy this.

    O: 저만 들었어요.
    E: Only I heard.

    O: 아침에는 커피만 마셔요.
    E: I only drink coffee in the morning.
    ㄹ: I only drink coffee _in the morning_.

    O: 아침에만 커피를 마셔요.
    E: I drink coffee[l], only in the mornings.

    O: 듣기만 했어요.
    E: I only listened (and did not e.g. talk).

    O: 보기만 할 거예요.
    E: I will only look (and not e.g. touch it).

    O: 오늘만 일찍 왔어요.
    E: I got here early only today.

    # 주문하다 - to order
    O: 맥주만 주문했어요.
    E: I only ordered beer.

    O: 왜 이것만 샀어요?
    E: Why did you only buy this?

    O: 어제 놀기만 했어요.
    E: I (did nothing but) only played yesterday.

    O: 영화는 집에서만 봐요.
    E: I watch movies only at home.
    F: I watch _movies_ only at home.

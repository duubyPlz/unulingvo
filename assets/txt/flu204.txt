== TTMIK 2.4 - And, with / -하고, -(이)랑 ==
* i -> informal

    O: 이거하고 이거 주세요.
    E: Give me this and this.

    O: 우유랑 빵
    E: milk and{i} bread

    O: 빵이랑 우유
    E: bread and{i} milk

    O: 우유하고 빵
    E: milk and bread

    O: 우유랑 빵 샀어요.
    E: I bought milk and{i} bread.

    O: 우유하고 빵 샀어요.
    E: I bought milk and bread.

    O: 빵이랑 우유 샀어요.
    E: I bought bread and{i} milk.

    # It is very unlikely that this sentence means “I watched a friend and a movie."
    O: 친구하고 영화 봤어요.
    E: I saw a movie with a friend.

    O: 누구랑 갔어요?
    E: Who did you go with{i}?

    O: 친구하고 같이 영화 봤어요.
    E: (I) saw a movie together with a friend.

    O: 누구랑 같이 갔어요?
    E: Who did you go with{i} together?

    O: 동생하고 공부할 거예요.
    E: I am going to study with my younger brother/sister.

    O: 선생님하고 밥을 먹을 거예요.
    E: I am going to eat rice/a meal with my teacher.

    O: 내일 선생님하고 경복궁에 갈 거예요.
    E: I am going to go to Gyeongbok Palace with my teacher tomorrow.

    O: 어제 홍대하고 신촌에 갔어요.
    E: I went to Hongdae and Sinchon yesterday.

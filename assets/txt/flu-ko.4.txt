== 4. 금방 / 방금 ==
* l -> long
* s -> short

    # 今方 - Just a minute ago, in no time
    # * 조금 후에, 금벙, 곧
    # * 조금 전에, 방금, 막
    # <https://hinative.com/questions/13721463>
    <https://twitter.com/ttmik/status/1071612965994950656>
    # > 금방 is more colloquial and has a more “immediate” feel
    O: 금방 오실 거죠?
    E: You will be coming soon, right?

    O: 금방 사무실에서 나갔어요.
    E: (He's) just stepped out of the office. 

    O: 금방 돌아올게요.
    E: I'll be right back.

    O: 방금 사당역을 지나쳤어요. 
    E: You just missed Sadang Station. 

    O: 그가 방금 내 차를 몰고 가 버렸어! 
    E: He just waltzed off with my car! @impolite

    O: 방금 보셨나요?
    E: Did you just see it? (Gently asking)

    O: 그들은 차를 몰고 왔어요.
    E: They came by car.

    O: 그는 차를 몰아 그녀의 집으로 갔어요.
    E: He drove to her house.

    O: 곧 돌아올게요.
    E: I'll be back right away.

    # > 막
    # * Just
    # * Recklessly
    # * Harshly
    O: 해가 막 지고 있었어요.
    E: The sun was just setting.

    O: 물이 막 끓으려고 해요.
    E: The water’s just about to boil.

    O: 이거는 집에서 막 입는 옷이에요.
    E: This{l} is just what I wear around the house.

    O: 글씨를 막 쓰다
    E: to write carelessly

    O: 그렇게 막 살다가는 언젠가 후회할 거예요.
    E: You will regret someday if you keep living recklessly like that.
    F: You will regret someday _if you keep living recklessly like that_.

    # 다루다 - to handle
    O: 그는 아랫사람을 너무 막 다뤄요.
    E: He is too harsh on those under him.
    F: _He_ is too harsh on those under him.

    O: 이거 소중히 다뤄주세요.
    E: Please handle this{s} with care.

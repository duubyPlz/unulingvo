== Occupation ==
> One

    O: 他不開心。
    E: He is not happy[c].

    O: 我是學生，你呢？
    E: I am a student, what about you?

    O: 他叫甚麼？
    E: What is he called?

    O: 我不是學生。
    E: I am not a student.

    O: 你不是學生。
    E: You are not a student.

    O: 他不是學生。
    E: He is not a student.

    O: 他不高興。
    E: He is not happy.

> Two
    O: 我們不吃魚。
    E: We do not eat fish.

    O: 他們是學生嗎？
    E: Are they students?

    O: 她喝茶。
    E: She drinks tea.

    O: 你們不吃飯嗎？
    E: Are you guys not eating?

    O: 我們喝水。
    E: We drink water.

    O: 他們很高興。
    E: They are happy.

    O: 你姓李嗎？
    E: Is your last name Li?

    O: 你們不吃飯嗎？
    E: Are you guys not eating?

    O: 他們不是學生。
    E: They are not students.

    O: 你們喝茶嗎？
    E: Do you guys drink tea?

> Three
    O: 她們不是老師。
    E: They[f] are not teachers.

    O: 他們不是醫生。
    E: They are not doctors.

    O: 他們是醫生。
    E: They are doctors.

    O: 醫生叫什麼？
    E: What is the doctor called?

    O: 你也是醫生嗎？
    E: Are you also a doctor?

    O: 老師姓什麼？
    E: What is the teacher's last name?

    O: 你是老師嗎？
    E: Are you a teacher?

== TTMIK 4.16 - Spacing / 띄어쓰기 #1 ==
* s -> short
* l -> long
* ll -> vlong
* e -> eng

    # {#RELATED Level 4 Lesson 21}: 띄어쓰기 #2

    # Rules (_ = space):
    # 
    # ## Spaces ##
    # S1. Adjective _ Noun
    # S2. Adverb _ Verb
    # S3. Noun Marker _ Verb
    # S4. Noun _ Noun
    # S5. _ Noun (in general)
    # 
    # ## No Spaces ##
    # N1. Noun|Pronoun Marker
    # N2. Proper nouns (no spaces anywhere)
    #
    # ## No Spaces (exceptions to Spaces)
    # E1. Common phrases (common in sino-korean words, but spaces with english words)
    # E2. Noun 하다

    # S1. Adjective _ Noun
    O: 부지런한 여자
    E: a hardworking woman

    O: 게으른 사람
    E: a lazy person

    O: 예쁜 강아지
    E: a pretty puppy
    
    O: 재미없는 사람
    E: a boring person (lit: A no-fun person)

    # S2. Adverb _ Verb
    O: 조용히 걷다
    E: to walk quietly

    O: 빨리 말하다
    E: to speak fast

    # S3. Noun Marker _ Verb
    O: 이거 샀어요.
    E: I bought this[s].

    O: 그것을 만들었어요.
    E: I made that[ll].

    # S4. Noun _ Noun
    O: 한국 여행
    E: trip to Korea (lit: Korean trip)

    O: 지갑 가격
    E: the price (of a) wallet

    # N1. Noun|Pronoun Marker
    O: 저는
    E: I + topic marker
    F: _I_ + topic marker @polite

    O: 저를
    E: I + object marker @polite

    # N2. Surname Firstname
    O: 최경은
    E: Choi Kyeong-eun

    O: 한국관광공사
    E: Korea Tourism Organization (lit: corporation) 🤔

    O: 서울도시철도공사
    E: Seoul Metropolitan Rapid Transit (lit: Seoul city railway corporation) 🤔

    # Exception 1. Common phrases (common in sino-korean words)
    O: 이것
    E: This thing[l]

    # Technically, 여자 친구 is correct
    O: 여자친구
    E: girlfriend

    # Exception 2. Noun 하다
    O: 공부하다
    E: to study

    O: 공부를 하다
    E: to study[l]

    O: 운동하다
    E: to exercise

    O: 운동을 하다
    E: to exercise[l]

    O: 청소하다
    E: to clean up

    O: 청소를 하다
    E: to clean up[l]

    O: 화장하다
    E: to put makeup on

    O: 수영하다
    E: to swim

    # Has a space = Because it is English
    O: 데이트 하다
    E: to date[e]

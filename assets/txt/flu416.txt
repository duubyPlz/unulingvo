== TTMIK 4.16 - Spacing / 띄어쓰기 ==
* s -> short

    # Rules (_ = space):
    # 
    # ## Spaces ##
    # S1. Adjective _ Noun
    # S2. Adverb _ Verb
    # S3. Noun Marker _ Verb
    # S4. Noun _ Noun
    # S5. _ Noun (in general)
    # 
    # ## No Spaces ##
    # N1. Noun|Pronoun Marker
    # N2. Surname Firstname

    # S1. Adjective _ Noun
    O: 부지런한 여자
    E: a hardworking woman

    O: 게으른 사람
    E: a lazy person

    O: 예쁜 강아지
    E: a pretty puppy
    
    O: 재미없는 사람
    E: a boring person (lit: A no-fun person)

    # S2. Adverb _ Verb
    O: 조용히 걷다
    E: to walk quietly

    O: 빨리 말하다
    E: to speak fast

    # S3. Noun Marker _ Verb
    O: 이거 샀어요.
    E: I bought this[s].

    O: 그것을 만들었어요.
    E: I made it.

    # S4. Noun _ Noun
    O: 한국 여행
    E: trip to Korea (lit: Korean trip)

    O: 지갑 가격
    E: the price of a wallet

    # N1. Noun|Pronoun Marker
    O: 저는
    E: I + subject marker
    F: _I_ + subject marker

    O: 저를
    E: I + object marker

    # N2. Surname Firstname
    O: 최경은
    E: Choi Kyeong-eun

    O: 한국관광공사
    E: Korea Tourism Organization 🤔

    O: 서울도시철도공사
    E: Seoul Metropolitan Rapid Transit 🤔

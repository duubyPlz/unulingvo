= TTMIK 2.21 - More.. than… / -보다 더 ==
    O: 더 빠르다
    E: to be faster

    O: 더 비싸다
    E: to be more expensive

    O: 더 예뻐요.
    E: It is prettier. / You are prettier. / She is prettier.

    O: 수박은 사과보다 더 커요.
    E: A watermelon is bigger than an apple.
    F: _A watermelon_ is bigger than an apple.

    O: 이거보다 더 커요.
    E: It is bigger than this one.

    O: 더 좋다
    E: to be nicer

    O: 더 좋아요.
    E: It is nicer.

    O: 이거보다 더 좋아요.
    E: It is nicer than this one.

    O: 더 착하다 
    E: to be nicer 

    O: 현우 씨는 더 착해요.
    E: Hyunwoo is nicer.
    F: _Hyunwoo_ is nicer.

    # 현우 씨는 경은 씨보다 착해요.
    O: 현우 씨는 경은 씨보다 더 착해요.
    E: Hyunwoo is nicer than Kyeong-eun. 
    F: _Hyunwoo_ is nicer than Kyeong-eun. 

    O: 오늘은 어제보다 더워요. 
    E: Today is hotter than yesterday.
    F: _Today_ is hotter than yesterday.

    O: 영어는 한국어보다 어려워요.
    E: English is more difficult than Korean.
    F: _English_ is more difficult than Korean.

    O: 어제보다 일찍 갈 거예요.
    E: I will go earlier than yesterday.

    O: 현정 씨가 저보다 더 잘해요
    E: Hyeonjeong is better than me (at doing that).
    F: Hyeonjeong _is better than me (at doing that)_.

    O: 저는 책을 읽는 것보다 사는 것을 더 좋아해요.
    E: I like buying books more than reading books.
    F: _I_ like buying books more than reading books.

== TTMIK 3.25 - Verb Ending / -네요 ==
    # Means:
    # * Impressed
    # * Surprised
    # * Own personal thought

    O: 크네요.
    E: (I see that) it is big. / (Oh, I did not know it was big, but) it is big. (Expressing surprise)

    # 잘 어울리다
    O: 잘 어울리네요
    E: Oh! I find that it looks good on you. (Expressing your impression)

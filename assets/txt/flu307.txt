== TTMIK 3.7 - / So / Linking Verbs With -아/어/여서 ==
* l -> long

    # Use cases:
    # 1. Reason
    # 2. Joining related actions
    # 3. Fixed expressions

    O: 비가 와서 못 가요.
    E: It is raining, so I cannot go.

    O: 비가 와서 못 갔어요.
    E: It rained, so I could not go.

    O: 오늘은 바빠서 영화를 못 봐요.
    E: I am busy today, so I cannot see the movie.
    F: I am busy _today_, so I cannot see the movie.

    # 반갑다 - to be glad to see someone
    # 만나서 반가워요.
    O: 만나서 반갑습니다.
    E: I met you, so I am glad. / It is nice to meet you. @formal

    O: 공원에 가서 책을 읽을 거예요.
    E: I am going to go to the park and read a book.

    # I met a friend, so (we) ate (together).
    O: 친구를 만나서 밥을 먹었어요.
    E: I met a friend, and (we) ate (together).

    O: 친구를 만나서 밥을 먹을 거예요.
    E: I am going to meet a friend, and (we will) eat together.

    # 모으다 - to save up
    O: 돈을 모아서 뭐 할 거예요?
    E: What are you going to do with the money you save up? (lit. You save up money and what will you do?)

    O: 케이크를 사서 친구한테 줄 거예요.
    E: I am going to buy a cake to/and give it to a friend.

    O: 계획에 따라서 진행하겠습니다.
    E: I will proceed according to the plan. @formal

    O: 예를 들어서
    E: for example

    O: 예를 들어서, 이렇게 할 수 있어요.
    E: For example, you can do it like this.

    O: 한국에 가서 뭐 할 거예요?
    E: After you go to Korea, what are you going to do?

    # Since I came to Seoul, I am glad.
    O: 서울에 와서 좋아요.
    E: I am glad to have come to Seoul.

    O: 술을 너무 많이 마셔서 머리가 아파요.
    E: I drank too much, so my head is aching.

    O: 비가 와서 집에 있었어요.
    E: It rained, so I stayed at home.

    O: 요즘에 바빠서 친구들을 못 만나요.
    E: These days I am busy, so I cannot meet my friends[l].

    O: 열심히 공부해서 장학금을 받을 거예요.
    E: I am going to study hard so I can get (and I will get) a scholarship.

    O: 한국어가 너무 재미있어서 매일 공부하고 있어요.
    E: Korean is so much fun that I am studying it every day.
    F: Korean _is so much fun that I am studying it every day_.

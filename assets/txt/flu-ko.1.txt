== 1. 얕 shallow ==
* s -> short
* e -> english
* l -> long

    O: 얕은 접시 
    E: A shallow dish

    O: 지식이 얕다.
    E: (Your) knowledge is shallow. @plain
    F: (Your) knowledge _is shallow_. @plain

    O: 넓고 얕은 구멍을 만들어 봐요.
    E: Let's try making a wide and shallow hole. @impolite

    O: 얕은 물 
    E: shallow water

    O: 수심이 얕아요.
    E: The water level's shallow.
    F: The water level_'s shallow_.

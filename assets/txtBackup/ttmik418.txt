== TTMIK 4.18 - Most, Best (superlative) / 최상급, 최고 ==
* f -> formal

    # Meanings:
    # * 제일 sino, spoken
    # * 가장 native, more formal
    # Most or best + adjective/adverb

    # More:
    O: 조금 더
    E: A little more

    O: 더 많이
    E: More (amount, adjective not just adverb)

    O: 돈 더 주세요.
    E: Give me more money.

    O: 더 마시고 싶어요.
    E: I want to drink more.

    # Most:
    O: 제일 좋아요
    E: (It) is the best.

    O: 제일 재미 있어요
    E: (It) is the most fun. / (It) is the most interesting.

    O: 제일 좋은 것
    E: the best thing

    O: 이게 제일 좋아요.
    E: This is the best.
    F: This _is the best_.

    # 제일 가까운 역이 어디에요? To where is the closest station?
    O: 제일 가까운 역이 어디예요?
    E: Where is the closest station?
    F: _Where is_ the closest station?

    O: 어떤 색깔이 가장 좋아요?
    E: Which color is the best{f}? / Which color is your favorite?
    F: Which color _is the best{f}_? / Which color is your favorite?

    # 村스럽다
    O: 촌스럽다
    E: not stylish / countrified

    O: 제일 먼저 온 사람이 누구예요?
    E: Who is the person that came here first?
    F: _Who is the_ person that came here first?

    O: 요즘 가장 인기 있는 가수는 누구예요?
    E: These days{s}, who is the most{f} popular singer?
    F: These days{s}, who is the _most{f} popular singer_?

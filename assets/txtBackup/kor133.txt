    # 싸우다
    O: 그들은 안 싸워.
    E: They do not fight.
    F: _They_ do not fight.

    O: 누가 지금 싸워?
    E: Who _fights now_?

    O: 나는 재미있는 게 있어.
    E: I{l} have something interesting.
    F: _I{l}_ have something interesting.

    O: 아이가 책을 시작해.
    E: The child starts the book.
    F: The child _starts the book_.

    O: 그녀의 가장 친한 친구는 몇 살이에요?
    E: How old is her best friend?
    F: How old is _her best friend_?

    O: 이건 어려워.
    E: This is difficult.
    F: _This_ is difficult.

    # (I) start now. # Without imperative
    O: 지금 시작해.
    E: Start now.

    O: 그는 어느 걸 먹어?
    E: Which one does he eat?
    F: Which one does _he_ eat?

    O: 그건 뭐야?
    E: What is that?
    F: What is _that_?

    O: 아기가 이걸 읽어.
    E: The baby reads this.
    F: The baby _reads this_.

    O: 학생들이 매일 학교에서 싸워.
    E: The students fight every day at school.
    F: The students _fight every day at school_.

    O: 너의 제일 친한 친구가 누구야?
    E: Who is your best friend?
    F: _Who is_ your best friend?

    O: 시작하자!
    E: Let's start!

    O: 이게 재미있어.
    E: This is fun.
    F: This _is fun_.

    E: I{l} have something interesting.
    F: _I{l}_ have something interesting.

    O: 이렇게 하면
    E: if you do it like this{l}

    O: 이러면
    E: if you do it like this{s}

    O: 저러면
    E: if you do it like that{s} (over there)

    O: 어떻게 해요?
    E: What{l} are we supposed to do? (lit: How should we do it?)

    O: 어떡해요?
    E: What{s} are we supposed to do?

    O: 어떻게 할 거예요?
    E: What{l} will you do? (lit: How are (you) going to do it?) / What are you going to do?

    O: 어떡할 거예요?
    E: What{s} will you do? / What are you going to do?

    O: 어쩔 거예요?
    E: What{ss} will you do? / What are you going to do?

    O: 이거 어떡할 거예요?
    E: What{s} will you do about this? / What are you going to do about this?

    O: 이거 어쩔 거예요?
    E: What{ss} will you do about this? / What are you going to do about this?

    O: 이제 어떡할 거예요?
    E: How{s} will you take care of it now? / Now what?
